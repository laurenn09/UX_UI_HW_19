Areas of Focus

I am passionate about being an advocate for the user from project start to finish. From Qualitative Interviews & Personas to Storyboards & Usability Testing, I deliver results with the user front and center every step of the way.

Problem solving is what we do, and I believe good collaboration is essential for finding solutions. Throughout the design process, I enjoy creative brainstorming sessions that allow for out-of-the-box ideas and concepts that are attune to user needs.

Creating beautiful, unique experiences that also provide solutions encompasses a considerable part of my drive for UX/UI design. Information architecture is an area I excel in. I also enjoy cultivating a mood and delightful setting for users, while staying in tune with accessibility standards.

My Work

AUSTIN CREATIVE ALLIANCE

COVID-19 + the Arts = endless dead ends. Our goal of this site redesign was to provide a creative space for artists to promote themselves digitally and network with other artists and potential supporters. In addition, we wanted to boost access to relief funding for today’s hardship, while planting seeds for ongoing support for the Austin arts community beyond the pandemic.

U.S. DEPARTMENT OF INTERIOR

The goal of this redesign was to completely restructure the navigation and provide pleasing aesthetics to ultimately give the user confidence that finding important information won’t be a headache, but a fun adventure that one could keep returning to.

TRAVEL APP DESIGN CHALLENGE

Despite the perks that the web brings for planning travel, the process has become confusing, frustrating, and disorganized, especially for those on a budget. The goal of this project was to develop an app for travelers on a budget that is fun and easy to use, with features such as: personalized itineraries with synced bookings, group collaboration, and pleasing aesthetics to make the travel planning process enjoyable and as stress-free as possible.
